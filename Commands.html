Games and Gambling Commands

  • 1. /gamble: Basic casino command, earn some cash!
  • 2. /trade <character card>: Trade any card from your deck with someone else's!
  • 3. /dm <user> <message>: Sends a message to the user *user must have DMs enabled*
  • 4. /view <#> or <character name>: View a Highly Valuable Character Card's info that you own!
